# Account & Data Deletion — Brainic

**App:** Brainic (developer: SB Studios)
**Last updated: 1 July 2026**

This page explains how to request deletion of your Brainic account and the data
associated with it.

## In the app (instant)
Open **Brainic → Profile tab → Delete account**, then confirm. This immediately
and permanently deletes your account and all associated data listed below.

## By email (if you've uninstalled the app)
Email us at **team.sb.studioz@gmail.com** from the address linked to your
Google sign-in, with the subject **"Delete my Brainic account"**. We will verify
the request and delete your data within **30 days**.

## What data is deleted
When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:
- Your **profile** — name, avatar, gender, friend code, XP, coins, level and game stats
- Your **friends list** and friend requests
- Your **leaderboard** entries
- Your **online match** and presence data
- Your authentication record (Google account link)

## What is kept
- Nothing personally identifying is retained after deletion.
- Anonymised or aggregated technical logs (if any) that cannot identify you may be
  retained for a limited period for security and reliability.

## Guests (no sign-in)
If you play as a guest without signing in, your progress is stored only on your
device. Uninstalling the app, or using **Settings → Reset game data**, removes it —
no request needed.
